Sweet Potato and Kale bowl

Variety of flavourls and tons of nutrients are all together in one bowl. With this easy recipe you can enjoy the meal.

Ingredients

For the sweet potatoes

  • 1 cup Sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup avocado
  • half cup olive oil
  • 1 tbs lemon juice
  • 1 cup quinoa

For the kale

  • 1 cup kale
  • 1 pinch salt
  • half tbs lemon juice
